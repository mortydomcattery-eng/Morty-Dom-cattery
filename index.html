Ethical breeder of Munchkin, Ragdoll, and Maine Coon kittens. Located in Bamenda, Cameroon.

Our Breeds

Munchkin Kittens

Playful, short-legged companions with huge personalities. Social and great with families.

Ragdoll Kittens

Gentle, blue-eyed, and affectionate. Known for going limp when held. Perfect lap cats.

Maine Coon Kittens

Large, fluffy, dog-like cats. Intelligent, gentle giants great with kids and other pets.
