Please Note: We do not offer unannounced visits to protect the health of our cats and kittens. All visits are by appointment only after an application is approved.

For kitten inquiries, please include which breed you’re interested in: Maine Coon, Ragdoll, or Munchkin.

Adoption Process

1. Initial Contact: Send us an email or message telling us about your home and which breed interests you.

2. Application: We’ll send you a adoption contract, which will show the kitten is legally yours.

3. Updates: You’ll receive weekly photos and videos of your kitten until go-home day.

4. Go Home: Kittens go home at 9-14 weeks after final vet check, vaccinations, and microchipping.
