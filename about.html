About Morty Dom Cattery

Dedicated to raising exceptional Maine Coon, Ragdoll, and Munchkin kittens

Our Story

Welcome to Morty Dom Cattery, a TICA registered cattery. We specialize in breeding healthy, well-socialized Maine Coon, Ragdoll, and Munchkin kittens that make wonderful family companions.

Our cattery is built on a foundation of love for these amazing breeds and a commitment to ethical breeding practices. Every kitten is raised in our home as part of our family, ensuring they are confident, affectionate, and ready to join yours.

Our Philosophy

Health First: All our breeding cats are DNA tested and screened for breed-specific conditions. Kittens receive comprehensive veterinary care before going home.

Early Socialization: Kittens are handled daily from birth and exposed to household sounds, children, and other pets to ensure they are well-adjusted.

Lifetime Support: We’re here for you long after your kitten goes home. Questions about care, behavior, or health? We’re just a message away.

TICA Registered

Morty Dom Cattery is proudly registered with The International Cat Association (TICA). This means we adhere to strict breeding standards and ethics. All our kittens come with TICA registration paperwork.
