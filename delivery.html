Delivery Information

Kitten Delivery

We offer safe, carefully planned delivery options throughout most of the United States — so distance is never a barrier to welcoming your perfect kitten home.

How It Works

Delivery Options

We work closely with each family to choose the safest and most convenient delivery method for both the kitten and the new owner. Options vary depending on location, distance, and budget.

🚗

Personal Delivery

For families within a reasonable driving distance, we may offer personal delivery, bringing your kitten directly to you in a safe, comfortable, climate-controlled environment. This is often the least stressful option for younger kittens.

🐾

Pet Transport Service

We partner with trusted, experienced pet transport services for mid-to-long distance deliveries. These professionals specialize in safe kitten transport and are carefully vetted before we trust them with our babies.

✈️

Airline Transportation

For long-distance or out-of-state deliveries, airline pet transportation may be arranged when appropriate. We only use airline options that meet our safety standards, and kittens are never shipped unless we are fully confident in their health and readiness for travel.

📍

Coverage Area

We deliver throughout most of the United States. Availability depends on transportation options, weather conditions, and airline or transporter routes. Contact us with your location and we'll find the best option for your area.

Paperwork

Documents That Travel With Your Kitten

Every kitten travels with the full paperwork needed for a smooth, verifiable handover.

  • Health certificate from a licensed veterinarian
  • Vaccination and deworming records
  • Adoption contract
  • Health guarantee
  • Feeding and care instructions
  • Registration papers (if applicable)

Communication

Updates During Delivery

We believe in keeping our families informed every step of the way — because waiting for your kitten should be exciting, not stressful.

  • Regular photo and message updates depending on delivery method
  • Tracking information provided whenever available
  • Direct line of communication with us throughout the journey
  • Confirmation when your kitten is safely on the way

Health & Safety

If Your Kitten Arrives Unwell

The health and safety of our kittens are our top priorities. Mild stress after travel can occasionally occur and is usually temporary — most kittens settle in quickly once they're in a warm, calm environment.

If your kitten arrives showing signs of illness, please contact us immediately and have the kitten examined by a licensed veterinarian. Our health guarantee policy will apply according to the terms outlined in the adoption agreement.

We never send a kitten we are not fully confident in. Every kitten is vet-checked and cleared before departure.
