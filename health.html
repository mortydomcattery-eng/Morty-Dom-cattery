Kitten Health & Guarantee

Raising healthy, happy kittens is our top priority

Our Health Protocol

Every kitten at Morty Dom Cattery receives comprehensive veterinary care before joining their forever home. We work exclusively with licensed veterinarians and follow strict health standards.

All Kittens Receive:

  • Complete veterinary health examination
  • First round of core vaccinations (FVRCP)
  • Deworming treatments at 2, 4, 6, and 8 weeks
  • Flea and parasite prevention
  • Microchipping for permanent identification
  • Veterinary health certificate for travel

Breed-Specific Testing

Ragdoll

  • HCM (Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy) DNA tested parents
  • PKD (Polycystic Kidney Disease) negative
  • FIV/FeLV tested negative

Maine Coon

  • HCM DNA tested parents
  • SMA (Spinal Muscular Atrophy) tested
  • PKD negative
  • Hip dysplasia screening
  • FIV/FeLV tested negative

Munchkin

  • X-rays to screen for lordosis and pectus excavatum
  • HCM screening for breeding cats
  • PKD DNA tested negative
  • FIV/FeLV tested negative
  • Joint and spine evaluations

Our 2-Year Health Guarantee

We stand behind the health of our kittens. Every kitten comes with a 2-year genetic health guarantee covering hereditary conditions including HCM, PKD, SMA, and breed-specific skeletal issues.

If your veterinarian diagnoses a covered genetic condition within 2 years, we will provide a replacement kitten or refund at our discretion. We require veterinary documentation and reserve the right to a second opinion.

Important: This guarantee requires that you maintain regular veterinary care and keep your kitten indoors. The guarantee is void if the kitten is bred, neglected, or allowed outdoors unsupervised.

Going Home Package

Your kitten will go home with everything needed for a smooth transition:

  • Complete vaccination and medical records
  • Signed health certificate from our veterinarian
  • Microchip registration paperwork
  • 5-day supply of current food
  • Blanket with mother and littermate scent
  • Favorite toy for comfort
  • Detailed care guide and feeding schedule
